Floppa (Russian шлёпа) is an online meme based on a karakali named Gosha (Russian гоша), also called Gregory. Gregory is less preferred and the meme community uses "floppa".
  • Where did the popularity of this karakal come from?

    Floppa on the internet appeared for the first time on December 23, 2019 on Instagram. The photo visible below was uploaded by a prozhony user. It is possible that the sympathies of Internet users this cat gained its large ears from which its name "big floppa" also comes from

  • Gosha family members:

    Owners - Andrey Bondariew and Jelena Bondariew

    Friend, half -zone Kot - Justin (Russian: жора, Zhora)

  • Floppa history

    He was born on December 21, 2017 in Karakala breeding in Kiev, Ukraine.

    He was transported from Kiev to Moscow with a minibus. The journey was very tiring for a cat.

    Floppa was not trust at first and contacted the owners only about food. Fortunately, they acclimatized.

    In 2020, Gosha underwent surgery to remove the tumor from behind his ear, but later recovered.

    In December 2021, Gosha and its owners appeared on the state Russian television 360.

    After Russia's attack on Ukraine Social Media in Russia was banned but the owners coped with it most likely VPNA and did not stop publishing content.
